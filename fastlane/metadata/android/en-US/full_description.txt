Water Sort is a simple yet addictive puzzle game where you sort colored water in tubes until each tube contains only one color.

How to play:
- Tap a tube to pick up the top color, then tap another tube to pour it.
- You can only pour the same color on top of another same color.
- Sort all the tubes to win.
